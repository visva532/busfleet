Our Story

From 5 Buses to 500+

What started as a small operation in 2015 has now grown into a national fleet that serves millions of passengers every year.

Built for Commuters

Our vision was clear from day one: make bus travel safer, smarter, and simpler. We crafted our platform to solve everyday commuter issues—from unpredictable schedules to hard-to-access bookings.

Tech-Driven Travel

With live bus tracking, app-based bookings, and route optimization algorithms, BusFleet has turned traditional travel into a seamless experience.

Eco-Conscious Choices

We’ve invested in electric and low-emission buses to cut carbon emissions and support sustainable urban mobility.

The Road Ahead

We are working toward AI-powered scheduling and integrated smart city transit systems — with the goal of making every ride timely, safe, and pleasant.
