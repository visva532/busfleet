Refund Policy

Effective Date: July 3, 2025

1. Ticket Cancellation

Passengers can cancel tickets up to 24 hours before departure to be eligible for a full refund. Cancellations made within 24 hours are subject to a 30% cancellation fee.

2. Refund Process

Approved refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days to the original payment method. Transaction charges, if any, may be deducted.

3. Delays and Cancellations by BusFleet

If we cancel a trip or delay it for more than 2 hours, passengers may opt for a full refund or reschedule at no extra cost.

4. Non-Refundable Cases

No refund will be issued for:

  • No-show at the departure point
  • Missed buses due to passenger error
  • Tickets booked through unauthorized agents

5. Contact Us

For refund-related queries, please contact us at refunds@busfleet.com or call +1-800-123-4567.
